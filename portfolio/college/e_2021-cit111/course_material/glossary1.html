Entity
Entity:

Any object (people, place, thing) that we want to model and store information about.
Attribute
Attribute:

Describes the facts, details or characteristics of an entity
Data
Data:

The bits and pieces, like numbers, characters or symbols with no context or meaning
Information
Information:

A set of data in context with relevance. At a certain place in time, or for a certain person the data makes sense.
Knowledge
Knowledge:

Information that has been acquired and gives understanding about the importance of the information. Used to make decisions.
