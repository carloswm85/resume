Overview

Final week. The students should have their challenges finished. They should also prepare a ~3 minute video reviewing how they evaluated themselves on the outcomes, and showing justfication from their portfolios for their self-ratings. Review their ratings, video, and if necessary portfolio. If you agree with the self-assessment then award that grade...if not adjust as necessary.

Remember that this grade will count for 70% of the final grade. The other 30% comes from the weekly readings and team activities.
