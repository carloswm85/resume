Snow in June? Only bad if you're a Californian YouTuber

It's been some crazy days here in Idaho. The National Weather Service in Pocatello, informed that last night they had 7 inches of snow falling during the night in the town. Boise didn't lack behing, where the National Weather Service informe 8 inches of snow falling during the same period of time. In the Tamarack Ski Resort (also in Boise, and at 6600 ft) registered 15 inches of snow.

"I didn't know it was even possible to have all this snow at this time of the year," some forecaster said to this site "I think I'm moving back to the coast next year." This was Willy Madison, a well known Californian YouTuber star who moved to Preston last summer looking for a more traquile life.

Strangely enough, just last weekend, the Boise area saw record temperatures that challenged all expectations for this time of the year. Too hot for this season, with tempratures as high as 104 ℉. "Is this Summer or Hell?", someone asked. "Oh, great! And now we have snow!", said the same person a week later.
