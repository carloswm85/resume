Target Audience

The target audience of the Hurricane Parrot site is described below:

  • WHO: It can be used by anyone who is looking to find out about the weather. It can be used by anyone who has access to the internet. Among other people, it could be used especially used by: Agriculturalists, policy makers, private sector.
  • WHERE: Visitors are from the state of Idaho, and from around the World.
  • WHEN: At any time of the day.
  • WHY: Because they are planning their day. People who visit this site can be locals, or anyone from outside of Idaho who wants to know the weather forecast for any given time of the month.

Scenarios

Scenarios describe the visitors purpose in visiting and using the site. Questions are made, of which the site content can provide the answers.

  • How will be the weather this morning/afternoon/evening?
  • Is going to snow/rain this weekend? I'm planning to have a barbecue with my friends.
  • How's going to be the weather next week on Idaho? I'm plannig to spend some days there.
  • I'm planning on seeding the land next week. Are we going to have the best weather conditions?
