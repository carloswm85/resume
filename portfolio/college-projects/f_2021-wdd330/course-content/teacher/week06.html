Web Frontend Development II

WDD 330

Instructor Preparation

Overview

No Team activity for this week. The students should spend almost all of their time working on their projects.

Meeting Time

This weeks meeting might be short. I would use it entirely for student questions if they bring them. Help them to finish their projects. Let them know your availability for meeting one on one with any of them that need help.

One other topic you may cover would be options for viewing their apps on a mobile device. Review how to setup github pages since many students are using it as a repository. You could also show them how to lookup IP addresses to make sure their computer and phone are on the same network, and using a tool like LiveServer in VS Code or the Brackets editor for a webserver connect the phone browser to the computer.
