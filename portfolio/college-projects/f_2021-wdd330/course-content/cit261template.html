Objectives

Getting Connected and Start Working

This week you will join a team and work with them to create your team schedule for the course. Feel free to pick any agile methodology you, as a team, would like. Use it to create your schedule. You can find a list of some agile methodologies here https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Agile_software_development#Agile_methods

Objectives

  • Meet and interact with your team.
  • Decide on an agile methodology your team will use.
  • Based on the methodology your team chooses, create a team schedule for achieving the fluency you want.
  • Begin working toward your schedule.
